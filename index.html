Space.A

Space.A is an educational website dedicated to bringing the wonders of space and exoplanets to curious minds. Whether you're a student, an enthusiast, or just a curious explorer, we provide interactive quizzes, detailed solar system insights, and an extensive glossary to help you understand the mysteries of the universe. Our mission is to make space exploration fun, accessible, and informative for everyone.

Features:

  • - Learn about 4500+ exoplanets
  • - Learn about our Solar System
  • - Interactive Fun Quiz with random question each time
  • - Easy to understand glossary
  • - Get a new Space Fact each time
